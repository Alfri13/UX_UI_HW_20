The Problem!

Bunny Buddies has an extensive website that contains important information regarding rabbit care, adoption information and donation requests. All the information fought for attention of the user and it was difficult to read.



The Solution!

Our team handled the task of organizing and prioritizing the information on the site. We wanted to keep most of the information since rabbit care and handling is a lot different than other pets, and we wanted to make sure our users had access to these resources.



The Process!

We conducted usability test on the site to see where pain points exsisted.





We then conducted user interviews as well as sent out a survey

And from that data, we synthesized a user persona.





The Prototype!

First we broke up the information into easier to read chunks. As shown in the example, we have removed the adoption form out of the body of the text and made it a call to action button.

We then added some color and made some adjustments to our image descriptions. We also bolded important information within the text.

We experimented with colors and how certain call to actions looked.

We also adjusted some layout designs to find what worked best.
